Confusion

The person is confused and doesn’t remember her name, address, phone number or why she is at a certain place.

What can I do:

  • Never show that you’re stressed since a person with dementia is extra sensitive to stressful situations.
  • Give information and instructions in a positive way.
  • Be friendly and calm.
  • Speak slowly and in a confident way.
  • Speak with a low voice

Read more under Tips on interaction.

Why is the person acting this way:

Confusion can be caused by sudden changes in behaviour, like anxiety, aggression, and hallucinations.
