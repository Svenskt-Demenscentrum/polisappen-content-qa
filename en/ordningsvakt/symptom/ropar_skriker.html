Shouting and screaming

The person yells out.

What can I do:

  • Take the person aside in order to reduce impressions.
  • Distract by trying to involve the person in an activity, like carrying a bag.
  • Take the person for a short walk, or do somthing else that gives new impressions.
  • If possible, distract by playing music in the phone.
  • Some like the comfort of holding hands.

Why is the person acting this way:

This behaviour might be an expression of feeling abandoned, a cry for help, or for a parent. It could also be a mechanical reaction or be caused by pain. The person who has dementia is not always aware of his behaviour.
