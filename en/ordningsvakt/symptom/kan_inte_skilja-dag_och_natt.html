Mixing up day and night

The person with dementia cannot tell day from night.

What can I do:

It’s no point trying to correct the person. Try to help her home or wherever she needs to be.

Why is the person acting this way:

Along with the progression of the disease, it can get increasingly hard for the person with dementia to keep track of time and tell day from night. When the damage in the brain has spread, it might feel completely logical to get up in the middle of the night, dress and go to work – even if the person stopped working years ago.
