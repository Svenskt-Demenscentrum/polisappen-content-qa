Society’s support

The municipality

Social services are in charge of social wellfare issues in the municipality or district. All municipalities have their guidelines for the way they work.

A needs assessor is the municipality official who assesses and handles cases according to the Social Services Act.

Home care services offer help at home. The person with dementia has to be willing to accept the help.

Day care units for people with dementia are mostly open during the day, Monday to Friday.

Nursing homes: Even if a person with dementia is not safe in her home, she can never be forced to move to a nursing home. She must accept and be willing to move. Family members can try to persuade her. Only in exceptional cases may custodial care be enforced.

The county

The district health care centre is where the person’s doctor, nurses, physiotherapist and occupational therapist work.

Home nursing can be offered by the district health care centre, if a person needs health care on a regular basis. This means that the person can get care in her home and that doctors and nurses are available day and night.
