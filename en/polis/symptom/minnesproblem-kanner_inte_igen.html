Memory problems – doesn’t recognise others

The person forgets, or is not capable of recognising, people or places, not even close friends or family. This could result in restlessness, anxiety or aggression.

What can I do?

  • If you ask a lot of questions that the person with dementia cannot answer, she might feel offended and be defensive.
  • Talk around the question you would like an answer to. It’s like detective work.
  • You can try to give clues that hopefully make the person come up with the answer from her memory. You could, for example, talk about his family: ”Maybe your children can help you.”
