What to do:

To break the apathy, try the following:

  • Encourage the person to participate.
  • Commend and acknowledge.
  • Use some humour, maybe try a joke.
  • If the person seems to like being close, touch her hand to make contact.
  • Ask one question at a time. Wait for the reply before you say something again.

Why does the person act this way:

This behaviour could be a side effect from medication or a depression. It could also be caused by pain or some other physical condition.
