Delusions

The person is upset that someone steals or hides things, that the partner is unfaithful, or believes that someone is trying to poison her.

What can I do:

  • Start by confirming what the person says and by doing that calm him down. Show that you take the problem seriously: ”What has disappeared? I will check it out.” Or: ”I have written this down and will look into it.”
  • You can also help the person look for whatever is missing.
  • After that you can distract him by starting to talk about something else.

Why is the person acting this way:

Delusions are something rather difficult that may appear at a later stage of the disease. Most of the time it’s not possible to persuade the person by using logical and rational explanations.
