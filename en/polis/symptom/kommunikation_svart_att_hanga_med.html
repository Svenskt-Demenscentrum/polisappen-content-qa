Communication – hard keeping up with conversation

The person has a hard time making conversation.

What can I do:

Make pauses when you speak. That way the person with dementia has a chance to keep up. Read more under Communication – the person doesn’t understand.

Why does the person act this way:

For the person with dementia it can get increasingly difficult to express herself. It’s up to us around her to make sure the meeting goes a smoothly as possible.
