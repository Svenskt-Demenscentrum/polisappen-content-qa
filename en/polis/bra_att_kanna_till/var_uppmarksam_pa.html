To look out for

Below are some signs of dementia to be aware of.

    Early signs are difficulties in:

  • communication
  • remembering dates and time
  • finding one's way, also in well-known areas
  • finding words
  • concentration
  • planning and carrying out an activity.

    The person might also:

  • act confused or anxious
  • repeat the same question several times
  • dress in an odd way
  • show unrestraint behaviour.

    At a later stage of the disease the person might:

  • live in the past
  • have a hard time recognising people
  • be restless and have a repetitive behaviour.
